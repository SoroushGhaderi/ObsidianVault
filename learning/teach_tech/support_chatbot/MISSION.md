# Mission: Support Chatbot Architecture

## Why
I want to gain general knowledge about the architecture of a support chatbot service to understand how such systems are designed, built, and scaled. As a backend developer, I aim to expand my understanding of system design, including backend services, AI/NLP integration, and deployment strategies.

## Success looks like
- I can explain the high-level components of a support chatbot system and how they communicate.
- I can identify key design decisions for backend services, AI integration, and deployment.
- I can evaluate trade-offs between different architectural approaches for a chatbot system.

## Constraints
- Backend developer background; prefer concrete examples and code snippets.
- General knowledge goal; no specific project or timeline.
- Learning style: structured lessons with interactive exercises.

## Out of scope
- Deep dive into specific NLP model training.
- Frontend UI/UX design details.
- Specific product implementations unless used as examples.
