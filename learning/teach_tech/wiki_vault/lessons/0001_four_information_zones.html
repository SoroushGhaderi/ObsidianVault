Lesson 1: Four Information Zones

Goal: classify information correctly before asking an LLM to write anything.

raw/ preserves evidence. wiki/ stores durable synthesis. journal/ captures the human activity stream. log/ records system operations.

Worked example

You read an article about Airflow sensors, then discover during work that a low poke_interval caused load:

  • The untouched article belongs in raw/.
  • The reusable sensor behavior belongs in wiki/concepts/.
  • The tool itself belongs in wiki/entities/airflow.md.
  • What happened today belongs in journal/2026-06.md.
  • The ingest operation belongs in log/2026-06.md.

Exercise

Classify each item before opening the answer:

  1. An original PDF about Kafka consumer groups.
  2. Your thought: “We may replace Kafka with Redpanda next quarter.”
  3. A stable explanation of consumer lag.
  4. “Codex created three pages from the Kafka PDF.”
  5. The accepted choice to retain Kafka this year.
Answer key
  1. raw/
  2. journal/ until it becomes a deliberate decision
  3. wiki/concepts/
  4. log/
  5. wiki/decisions.md
